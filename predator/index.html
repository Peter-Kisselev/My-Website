Predators
is a cellular automata based on Conway's Game of Life ( Wikipedia article ).
But, there is a twist. In predators the classic cells that are alive are called prey, and there is a new type of cell: the predator cell. While the prey is the same as a living cell in the game of life, the predator cells are a completely different story. A predator cell will move randomly around the board until it spots at least one prey cell, then it randomly eats one of the prey cells that it sees.
